Where to Stay

Hotels & Accommodation

Stay in Sorsogon

Accommodation for every traveller

From comfortable city hotels in Sorsogon City to rustic eco-lodges in Donsol and beachside guesthouses in Matnog, the province offers options for all budgets.

Sorsogon City Hotels

The widest range of accommodation — mid-range business hotels, family inns, and apartelles with complete amenities.

Donsol Eco-Lodges

Comfortable eco-friendly lodges near the whale shark area. Most offer packages including boat tours and meals.

Beach Resorts (Matnog)

Budget beach resorts and camping areas near Subic and Tikling beaches for island-hoppers and nature seekers.

Mountain Lodges (Irosin)

Near Bulusan Natural Park, mountain bed-and-breakfasts accommodate trekkers and birdwatchers at accessible rates.

Booking Tip

During peak whale shark season (February–April) and Kasanggayahan Festival (October), accommodation fills quickly. Book at least 4–6 weeks ahead and confirm directly with properties for current rates.
