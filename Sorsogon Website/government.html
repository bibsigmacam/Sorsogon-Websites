Government

Administration

Provincial government

Sorsogon is governed by an elected Governor and Vice-Governor, supported by a Sangguniang Panlalawigan (Provincial Board). The provincial government is headquartered in Sorsogon City.

The province belongs to Region V (Bicol Region) under the national government's administrative structure. Locally, 16 cities and municipalities each have their own elected mayor, vice-mayor, and council managing local services and ordinances.

Congressional Districts

Sorsogon is divided into two congressional districts. The 1st District covers northern and western municipalities; the 2nd District covers southern and eastern municipalities including Sorsogon City.
