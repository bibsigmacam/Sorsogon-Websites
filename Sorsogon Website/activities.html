Things to Do

Activities & Adventures

Adventures

There is always something to do

Marine encounters, volcanic treks, cultural immersion, or simply watching the sunset from a pristine shore — Sorsogon delivers experiences for every kind of traveller.

Whale Shark Swimming

The Sorsogon bucket-list experience. Snorkel alongside whale sharks in Donsol Bay from November to June.

Surfing — Gubat

Punta Beach offers consistent Pacific swells from October to March. Board rentals and lessons available locally.

Volcano Trekking

Hike through dense rainforest to Bulusan's crater rim. Trails range from easy lakeside walks to challenging summit routes.

Island Hopping

Explore Subic Beach, Juag Lagoon, and Tikling Island by bangka from Matnog — each island uniquely beautiful.

Lake Kayaking

Paddle across the glassy surface of Bulusan Lake surrounded by volcanic forest. Kayaks available at the park entrance.

Firefly Watching

Evening river trips through mangroves illuminated by thousands of synchronous fireflies along the Ogod River.

Snorkeling & Diving

Coral gardens around Matnog and the Pacific coast offer rich reef ecosystems and diverse fish life year-round.

Hot Spring Soaking

Soak in natural geothermal pools at San Benon or Buhang Hot Springs after a satisfying day on the trails.

Fishing Trips

Join local fishermen on pre-dawn fishing expeditions in the open Pacific — authentic and unforgettable.

Cycling

Explore Sorsogon by bicycle along the coastal highway and rural farm roads. Rentals available in the city.

Cooking Classes

Learn to prepare kinunot, laing, and pinangat with local families and community-based cooking programs.

Photography Tours

Bulusan at sunrise, the Kasanggayahan parade, and Matnog's beaches offer extraordinary photographic opportunities.
